# Heart Health Scorecard
Assess cardiovascular stress from real-time biometrics during work.
When HR exceeds resting baseline by 30%+, flag acute cardiovascular stress.
When HRV drops below personal baseline, indicate sympathetic nervous system dominance.
Recommend: immediate vagal tone activation (cold water on wrists, box breathing 4-4-4-4).
If sustained elevated HR + low HRV for 2+ hours, recommend stepping away from screen.
Track resting HR trend over the week — rising resting HR indicates accumulated stress.
Provide composite score: HR variability + resting HR trend + recovery rate post-meeting.
