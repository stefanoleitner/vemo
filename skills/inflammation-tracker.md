# Inflammation Tracker
Detect chronic inflammation patterns from sustained workplace stress.
When stress markers are elevated for 3+ consecutive days, flag inflammation risk.
Cross-reference: poor sleep + high HR + low HRV = inflammation cascade.
Recommend anti-inflammatory protocol: hydration, omega-3 intake, reduce caffeine.
If HRV trend is declining week-over-week, recommend a full recovery day.
Suggest Mediterranean-style meals, prioritize sleep over late-night work.
Flag when meeting load exceeds 5 hours/day for 3+ days as inflammation trigger.
Retest protocol: reassess markers after 7 days of intervention.
