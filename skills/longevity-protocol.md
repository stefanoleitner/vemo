# Longevity Protocol
Frame workplace stress in the context of long-term health outcomes.
Chronic high cortisol accelerates biological aging — quantify the impact.
The four pillars: Exercise (150+ min/week), Sleep (7-9h), Nutrition, Emotional Health.
When recovery is consistently low, recommend Zone 2 cardio (walking, easy cycling).
Sustained HRV depression correlates with accelerated cardiovascular aging.
Recommend: daily movement breaks (5 min/hour), standing desk alternation.
Evening protocol: no screens 1h before bed, 10-min meditation, cool room (65-68F).
Weekly check: compare biological age markers against chronological age.
Long-term intervention: schedule quarterly health assessments.
